NightRate

Make the most of off-peak electricity tariffs. Enter your appliances and cheap-rate hours — NightRate tells you exactly when to run them and what delay to set.

SleepWindow

Work backwards from your wake-up time to find a better bedtime. Choose when you want to wake up and SleepWindow calculates three bedtime options based on 6, 5, or 4 sleep cycles — with a small allowance for the time it takes to fall asleep. The goal is to wake at the end of a cycle, not in the middle of one.
