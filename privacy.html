Privacy Policy

NightRate  ·  Last updated April 2026

Overview

NightRate is designed to work without collecting any personal data. This policy explains what that means in practice.

Data we collect

We collect nothing. NightRate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data stored on your device

The app stores your appliance settings and tariff times locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-party services

NightRate does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. No data is shared with third parties.

Account creation

NightRate does not require an account. No sign-up, no login, no email address.

Children

NightRate does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
