Privacy Policy

Jaques Lab · Last updated April 2026

Overview

All apps made by Jaques Lab are built on the same principle: they do what they say, locally, without collecting anything about you. This policy covers every app published under the Jaques Lab name.

Data we collect

None. Jaques Lab apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no analytics, no usage tracking, and no crash reporting sent anywhere.

Data stored on your device

Some apps store your settings or preferences locally on your device — for example, appliance configurations or time format preferences. This data stays on your device and is never accessible to us.

Third-party services

Jaques Lab apps do not integrate with third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers. No information is shared with any third party.

Accounts

None of our apps require an account. No sign-up, no login, no email address.

Children

Our apps do not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the date at the top of this page will be updated. We'll only update it if something meaningful changes, which given the above, would be unusual.
